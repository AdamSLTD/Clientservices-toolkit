Sales Disqualifiers

Not Available on Any Channels:

  • Create/sync customer contacts from another platform
  • PCI/HIPPA compliance (Nothing on public roadmap)
  • Add different time-zones for different teams
  • Sync status between Talkdesk and Omnichannel (Roadmap 2020)
  • Co-browsing (Roadmap 2020)
  • Agent Interface in another language (Only available in English but Agents can respond in any unicode and bidirectional language they like)
  • Open PDF attachments without downloading a Chrome extension (Requires pdf reader extension downloaded to Chrome)
  • Search historical chats by subject name or keywords from the body of the message (This is not possible because IMI encrypts
    subject line and email content to secure sensitive PII data)
  • Calling, payments, plug-ins/stickers in social apps
  • Transfer chats to an agent who’s offline/unavailable
  • Receive an alert when an agent has escalated an issue

Not Available on Email:

  • Include conversation history in the email when an agent responds to a customer’s email
  • Reply All and CC for the email channel
  • Link a shared mailbox (No SMTP details) to Omnichannel
  • Display the full body of an email without having to click ‘Preview’

Not Available on Livechat:

  • Add screenshots to the body of a message
  • Have the title and byline text to be fully displayed when I scroll down to the bottom of the widget

Not Available on SMS:

  • Send MMS
  • Use non Twilio Numbers
  • Remote call forward to SMS numbers
  • Use both Salesforce SMS and Omnichannel on the same Twilio number

Requires Custom Development:

  • Chatbot
  • Any CRM Interactions/Automations
  • Automatically route interactions based on keywords
  • Filter out interactions and automatically close them
  • Auto-tag chats
  • Auto-flag chats
  • Append a signature to a conversation
