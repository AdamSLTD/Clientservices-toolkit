Overview


Talkdesk 'Wow Demos' encompass common pain points and workflows that customers face while following powerful user stories.

  • The Convoy Demo lives in Talkforce and showcases the power of Talkdesk Omnichannel. Sign into Talkforce and set yourself available as an agent in the convoy chat team while also navigating to the convoy page in the drop-down menu above. Sending messages in the chat widget at the bottom right of the page will spawn a message in the Omnichannel queue where you can grab the conversation and perform a live interaction.
